Government’s Fiscal Balance Report: The First half of Fiscal Year 2018
The government has received 1,018,120 million baht in revenue. The government budget disbursement was at 1,598,010 million baht. To finance such deficit, the government has borrowed 285,408 million baht.
Ms. Kulaya Tantitemit, Inspector General of Ministry of Finance and Ministry of  Finance’s Spokesman, announced the government fiscal balance for the first half of fiscal year 2018 (October 2017 - March 2018). The government has received 1,018,120 million baht in revenue. The government budget disbursement was at 1,598,010 million baht. To finance such deficit, the government has borrowed 285,408 million baht. Therefore, the treasury reserve at the end of March 2018 was at 262,555 million baht.
Ms. Kulaya concluded, “Fiscal balance in the first half of fiscal year 2018 remained strong, due to the sufficient level of treasury reserve to support budget disbursement that will accelerate in the second half of fiscal year 2018.”
